The Lifestyle

Everything You Need.
All in One Place.

Lee County offers a rare combination of natural beauty, growing economy, excellent schools, and affordable living — all under the Florida sun.

🌴

Natural Beauty

Miles of pristine beaches, state parks, nature preserves, and waterways. Lee County is where Florida's natural splendor is at its finest.

📚

Great Schools

Lee County School District serves over 90,000 students with numerous A-rated schools, magnet programs, and strong extracurricular offerings.

💼

Growing Economy

Major employers across healthcare, technology, tourism, and construction are driving job growth and economic opportunity throughout the region.

🏖️

Beach Living

Fort Myers Beach, Sanibel Island, and Cape Coral are all within easy reach. World-class beaches are practically in your backyard.

🏥

Healthcare

Lee Health operates multiple hospitals and hundreds of care locations throughout the county, providing excellent healthcare access for residents.

🛣️

Connectivity

Southwest Florida International Airport, Interstate 75, and US-41 make Lee County accessible to the rest of Florida and beyond.
